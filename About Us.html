What is Winger Bedding?

Winger Bedding is a registered company in Zimbabwe which does the manufacturing and selling of bedding materials. Products on sale are blankets, towels, fleece, pillows .etc.

Where is it based?

Winger Bedding is currently based in Norton Zimbabwe but not confirned to that location when it comes to business. The company networks with other companies together with various individuals in delivering goods and services.
